Personal Projects

Cryptotrage

I built a digital currency trading bot. Back when markets for digital currencies were less efficient and volatile, statistical arbitrage could be used to profit from certain currency exchanges discrepancies. Using developer APIs to get trade information, my bot found and forecast mispricings. This was a scary bot to run for a 19 year old with little liquidity; this notion of conducting arbitrage in cryptocurrency circles is outdated due to increased players in the market and long transaction times.

More recently, I acted as a Marker Marker in a few low volume cryptocurrency markets. I was able to consistently find bid-ask spreads representing up to ~20% of market price.

I am now working with Gavin Andresen (Bitcoin's ex lead developer) on the Random Sanity Project, to find simple ways for comapnies to ensure they are running secure software.
